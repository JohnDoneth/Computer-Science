What is EMS?

The Event Management System is a suite of programs and applications that work together to provide a comprehensive event experience for organizations such as FIRST, FIRST Global, or Battlebots. Questions? Check our our FAQ.

Why EMS?

EMS was built by the community, for the community. With customizable options ranging from audience display icons to event administrative tasks, EMS is ready to tackle your event's every need. Check out its features.

How?

Powerful web technologies such as TypeScript, React, Electron, and Express all fuel EMS, which is what makes it cross-platform. Interested in contributing?
