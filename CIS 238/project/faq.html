FAQ

FAQ

How reliable is EMS?

EMS is built upon technologies used by today's industries, and has proven itself in large-scale events such as FIRST Global.

How easy is it to use EMS?

EMS may appear very large and complex at first, but documentation and live support is available. Contact us for live support at your event.

How often does EMS receive updates?

The development cycle of EMS is constant. New features are always being discussed and implemented. Bugs are being discovered, and we make it our goal to ship as close to perfect software as we can.

Can I use EMS for my official FTC event?

No. EMS is not the official scoring system for FIRST Tech Challenge. However, EMS may be used for scrimmages, offseasons, and other events that are NOT official FIRST events.
