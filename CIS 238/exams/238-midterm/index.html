Doggie Daycare of West Michigan

Daycare

We offer customized daycare for your pet. We treat your pet to fun-filled days of socializing with other dogs, interacting with our caring team, and exercising. New clients receive complimentary day of care to introduce your pet to their new surroundings. Call ahead for availability because spots are limited. Dog daycare services includes:

  • Webcams
  • Nap, lunch and/or treats
  • Grooming

Boarding

Sometimes work or vacation takes you away from your furry loved one. Our friendly and attentive team will do everything we can to ensure your dog feels at home away from home. Whether they need overnight, weekend, holiday or extended care, your dog will spend days enjoying supervised open play with their furry friends.

Our staff is available 24 hours a day, so pets get all the attention they need. You can trust us! We love dogs!
